### paytm-health-analysis
Financial health and risk analysis of Paytm using Yahoo Finance data, focusing on liquidity, leverage, cash flow, and market volatility.

## Key Metrics Analyzed
- Liquidity: Current Ratio
- Leverage: Debt-to-Equity
- Profitability: ROE, Operating Margin
- Cash Flow: Operating & Free Cash Flow
- Market Risk: Price volatility

## Key Insights
- Strong asset base with low debt
- Negative operating cash flow indicates profitability pressure
- High stock price volatility reflects market uncertainty
- Equity remains the primary funding source
